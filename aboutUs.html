ABOUT US

Who: Interlake Girls who Code, a group of high school girls who love to code.
Where: Portable 1 of Interlake High School, Bellevue, Washington
When: Fridays 3:30 to 4:30
Why: To educate girls in the realm of website coding
How: Using FreeCodeCamp to educate and GitHub to create
Managers: Bianca Greydinger, Elaine Guo, and Hannah Jeon
Languages: HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Our goal is to educate Interlake girls about website coding, because coding is often a huge part of the business world. We focus primarily on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as the websites used in everyday life are products of these languages. We hope to inspire girls to change the digital world using the knowledge gained.
