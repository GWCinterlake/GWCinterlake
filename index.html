Mission

We aim to inspire girls to pursue STEM careers by educating them and equipping them with skills they can use in the future.

In the tech industry today, there is a severe disparity between women and men in the workforce. The ratio between men and women in STEM is about 3:1, and women get paid approximately 15% less than men. It is time for a culture change, and for men and women to become more equal in the workforce.
